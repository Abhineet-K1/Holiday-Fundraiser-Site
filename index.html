About Us

Our mission is to spread joy and holiday cheer throughout our community by baking and delivering delicious homemade cookies through door-to-door sales. This fundraiser is dedicated to bringing neighbors together, sharing sweet moments, and supporting the SickKids foundation. With every cookie purchased, we aim to make a meaningful impact, foster kindness, and celebrate the spirit of giving this holiday season.


Where are we Located?

While our online services span accross Brampton, our door to door sales are only hosted around the Williams Pkwy and Mississauga Rd area.


What Inspires Us?

As illness continues to ruin the lives of many, poverty inevitabley riddles the world as well. As a result of this some gaurdians are unable to afford treatment for their children. I've witnessed this single handedly in close friends and with the combination of my will to help people and love for the holiday season, what better way to help support the ill than a baking fundraiser!

Events


We host multiple events around the year usually on holidays to celebrate the occasion and to spread the Joy to those in need. Whenever an event has been planned out, it will be posted here so be sure to check and sign up by clicking whichever event you would be interested in joining.
